Key Benefits

Harness the power of your data and access world-class asset management expertise.

FORECAST PERFORMANCE

Understanding how your pavement will behave is the key to planning. Pavy helps you apply deterioration rates to current pavement condition to predict future performance.

MANAGE MAINTENANCE

Airfield pavements are complex and extensive. Pavy gives you the right information and insight to control where and how to maintain them.

OPTIMIZE INVESTMENT

The key to asset management is balancing cost, risk and performance. Pavy provides targeted investment plans to help you maximize your ROI.

The story so far

The asset management principles behind Pavy were originally conceived by Heathrow Airport who strive to excel in how they manage their airfield pavements.

Jacobs produced the models and developed PAVY to support the management of Heathrow’s 4 million m2 of airfield pavement.

Jacobs has developed Pavy further for two other UK airports to advance PAVY’s capabilities.
